# Sales Analysis
****Sales Performance and Strategic growth Insights for Blinkit's Quick Commerce Operations****


![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/29c322ea-36df-411e-9020-4d4a350f00bf)




**Sales Breakdown:**
* Item types:
  - Highest sales are from Fruits and vegetabels, and Snacks Food(around $0.3M).
  - Followed by Household and Frozen Foods.

* Outlet Type:
  - Grocery Store outlets have the highest total sales(~$151.54k) but also the lowest average item rating(3.3).
  - Supermarket Type1 and Supermarket Type2 contribute heavily, with better item visibility and ratings.

* Outlet locations:
  - Sales are quite evenly spread across Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 cities.
 
**Fat Content Impact:**
* Items labeld as Low Fat dominated sales compared to Regular Fat items.

**Sales trend Over Years(Outlet Establishment):**
* A growth trend is visible from 2010 to 2015.
* Peakes around 2014 and 2015, indicating expansion of outlets or more sales from newer outlets.

**Outlet Size:**
* Medium-sized oulets (with sales of ~$444.79K) performs significantly better than small or high-sized outlets.

**Other Observations:**
* Supermarket Type1 and Type2 oulets have better avaerage ratings compared to others.
* Item Visibility metrics seems to vary slightly but stays relatively low across all outlets types(~0.05 to 0.08 range).


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**Actionable Startegies**

1. Focus More on Fruits, Vegetables and Household Items
   - Since these categories are already leading sales, BlinkIt sholud:
     * Expand product variety in these categories.
     * Offer bundles(e.g, Combo deals: fruits + vegeatbles pack).
     * Promote these itmes in ads and banners more heavily.

2. Boost Low-Fat Item Promotion
   - Customers prefer Low Fat products(higher sales share).
   - Startegies:
     * Highlight "Low Fat" labels in app listings.
     * Create "Healthy Picks" sections.
     * Offer discounts on low-fat variants to drive even more sales.
    
3. Improve Average Ratings for Grocery Stores
   - Grocery stores have the lowest ratings(~3.3) compared to supaermarkets.
   - Actions:
     * Quality checks for popular grocery outlets.
     * Implement better packaging and faster delivery.
     * Offer customser service follow-ups for low-rated deliveries.
    
4. Invest More in Tier 1 and Tier 2 Cities
   - Sales from Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities are strong and stable.
   - BlinkIt can:
     * Launch targeted promotions like free delivery weekends.
     * Partner with local suppliers to widen product range.
     * Open micro-fulfillment centers for quicker deliveries.
    
5. Expand Medium Outlet Sizes
   - Upgrade small outlets to medium-sized hubs where possible.
   - Encourage popular oulets to increase inventory and stock variety.
  
6. Strengthen Product Visibility
   - Visibility scores are quite low(~ 0.06 avaerage)
   - BinkIt can:
     * Optimize search and recommendation algorithms.
     * Improve app UX(filters, tags like "Best Seller" or "New Arrival").
     * Provide ad-space opportunities for selleers to increase their product visibility.
